Site Plan - Real Estate Management

HomeHub Rentals

HomeHub Rentals is a centralized platform for managing house rentals and real estate properties, with emphasis on the convenience and comprehensive services offered.

Site Purpose

The purpose of HomeHub Rentals is to provide a comprehensive platform for landlords and tenants to manage rental properties efficiently. The site will offer services such as property listings, tenant screening, rental payment processing, and maintenance request management.

Scenarios

Scenario 1: How can I list my property for rent on HomeHub Rentals?

Scenario 2: Where can I find information about the rental application process?

Scenario 3: How do I submit a maintenance request for my rental property?
